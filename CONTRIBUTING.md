# Contribution Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Awesome Rices! We appreciate contributions from the community. To showcase your unique rice, please follow these guidelines:

## Share Your Rice

1. **Prepare your rice repository:** Ensure the `README.md` of your rice repository includes screenshots of your desktop environment. 

2. **Create a Pull Request (PR):** Add an entry for your rice under the correct WM/DE section in our `README.md` using the following format:

    ```markdown
    ### [Your GitHub Username](https://github.com/yourusername/yourrepositoryname)
    [![Your GitHub Username](https://github.com/yourusername/yourrepositoryname/raw/main/path_to_your_image.png)](https://github.com/yourusername/yourrepositoryname)
    ```


   ##### Entries should be sorted alphabetically to ensure order and easy navigation for users.
